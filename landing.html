Why CUBEChinese?

CUBEChinese follows an interdisciplinary approach. In addition to Chinese language learning, we expose students to a wide range of subjects such as critical thinking, science, humanities, art, and sociology. We build an immersive Chinese learning environment that is in line with the cognition of young learners and encourages students to broaden their perspective, as well as spark their curiosity and desire to explore.

  • Interdisciplinary Chinese learning
  • Immersive Chinese learning experience
  • Game-based and interactive course settings
  • Supplementary pre-class nursery rhymes, post-class review videos and assignments
  • Officially accredited and certified teachers with personalized teaching strategy
  • Authoritative academic standard and evaluation mechanism

CUBEChinese curriculumis structured in accordance with leading academic standard - ACTFL, HSK, YCT, Chinese National Curriculum Standard (部编版语文), PIRLS, CCSS, CEFR language international standard and Piage's theory of cognitive development. Immersive teaching methodology is strictly followed to comprehensively help children transit into Chinese learning easily.

CUBEChinese programsetting respects the uniqueness of each child. We tailor-made diverse learning programs for students based on different language proficiencies, learning goals, and learning curves.

Preschool Immersion Program

Designed for students who are studying Chinese as a second language and weak in speaking and listening.
The program syllabus is based on YCT, and Happy Chinese (《快乐汉语》), integrating daily vocabularies and sentences to build up an immersive Chinese speaking environment and to help students transit into Chinese learning easily.

International Immersion Program

Suitable for students with fundamental speaking and listening skill and who are interested in learning pinyin, common Chinese characters and sparkling of curiosity in traditional culture.
The program is aligned with YCT exam syllabus and aiming to improve communicational skill from Chinese characters to words and phrases. The syllabus covers stroke order, pinyin, and simplified classic literatures.

Foundation & Enrichment Program

Suitable for students with strong listening and speaking skills and focusing on enhancing Chinese characters recognition and strengthening their understanding of traditional Chinese culture.
The program integrates Chinese characters, idioms, and fables to help students understand the transformation of Chinese characters and develop in-depth understanding of Chinese history and traditional culture.

Literature & Advanced Program

Suitable for students with strong listening and speaking skills and able to read and write more than 300 Chinese characters, expecting to advance in comprehensive reading and writing composition.
The program is focusing on classic literature reading and comprehensively improves students' reading skills from article understanding to innovation. Thematic essay writing stimulates students' interest in creative writing. The syllabus is aligned with 部编版 《语文》syllabus set by the China Ministry of Education.​
